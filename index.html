Research

Cultural evolution & bioacoustics — University of Oxford

I study how culture evolves in wild bird populations at Oxford's Edward Grey Institute. My work combines large-scale acoustic monitoring with machine learning to understand how bird song is transmitted and changes across generations.

21,283 hours of recordings · 85,000+ songs · 950,000+ notes · 430 birds

With Ben C. Sheldon (Oxford) and Sara Keen (Cornell Lab of Ornithology)
